Ineffable

Definition:

too great or extreme to be expressed in words



Examples:

  1. The beauty of the night sky in the mountains was ineffable.
  2. He was overwhelmed with an ineffable sense of joy after seeing his child for the first time.
  3. The feeling of loss she carried was ineffable, too deep for language.

Synonyms and Antonyms:

Synonyms Antonyms
