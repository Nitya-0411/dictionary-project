Ephemeral

Definition:

Lasting for a very short time.



Examples:

  1. The cherry blossoms are ephemeral, blooming brilliantly for just a few days each year
  2. Fame on social media is often ephemeral, disappearing as quickly as it comes
  3. She captured the ephemeral glow of sunrise in her painting.

Synonyms and Antonyms:


Synonyms Antonyms
fleeting permanent
transitory lasting
