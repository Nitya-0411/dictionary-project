Serendipity

Definition:

fortunate discovery by chance



Examples:

  1. Their friendship was a product of serendipity, formed after missing the same train.
  2. Many scientific breakthroughs are born out of serendipity rather than careful planning.
  3. It was pure serendipity that I found my lost ring in the garden.

Synonyms and Antonyms:


Synonyms Antonyms
coincidence misfortune
fluke accident
